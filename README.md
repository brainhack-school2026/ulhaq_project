## About me

I'm doing my Masters at the Institute of Medical Science, in the Interventional Psychiatry Lab at Sunnybrook Research Institute. My research sits at the intersection of metabolic psychiatry and neuromodulation. I'm currently working on a retrospective analysis of eeg data, a project integrating structural MRI data, as well as a trial using a ketogenic diet adjunctively with intermittent theta-burst stimulation (iTBS), all for a treatment-resistant depression population.
